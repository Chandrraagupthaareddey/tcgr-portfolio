01   About

From hospitality
to SRE to
AI ops.

My career didn't start in engineering. It started in hotel revenue analysis — staring at spreadsheets, spotting patterns in noise, and building automations to escape repetition. That instinct never left.

Over 15 years, I've evolved from automating night audits with Google Apps Script to architecting multi-LLM AIOps platforms for Airbnb Payments — combining classical SRE practice with modern AI reasoning to detect, triage, and resolve incidents with minimal human overhead.

I'm fluent across Datadog, Grafana, Prometheus, Splunk, ELK, Telescope — and equally at home orchestrating Claude, GPT, and Gemini through MCP servers and prompt chains. The interesting work today lives at the intersection: observability that thinks.

I write code to remove work, not to make more of it. I optimize for systems that explain themselves. And I believe the best engineers spend more time deleting alerts than creating them.
